Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is layered with rich meat sauce, creamy cheese and tender pasta.

It is a warm and perfect dish for family meals.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (flat sheets of lasagna);
  • Ground beef;
  • Tomato sauce;
  • Mozzarella cheese;
  • Ricotta cheese.

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions;
  2. Prepare the meat sauce with ground beef and tomato sauce;
  3. Layer noodles, sauce and cheese in a baking dish;
  4. Bake in the oven at 190Â°C until golden and bubbly.
